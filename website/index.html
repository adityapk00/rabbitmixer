Rabbit Mixer is a Trustless
Confidential-Transactions based Ethereum Mixer.

Hidden Addresses and Amounts

Stealth Addresses and RingCT hide addresses and amounts on the blockchain.

Transact without Gas

Transact by signing messages, removing the need acquire Ether to pay for gas. Completely unlink Ether.

Reasonable Fees

RingCT hides amounts even from the mixer, so pay only a reasonable, flat fee to use the mixer.
